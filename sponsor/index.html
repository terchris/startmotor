About



Startup Weekend Oslo is aimed at developers, designers, marketers and innovators. They are people who love to create and build. They are people who make things happen.

We also engage external support providers in the form of coaches, judges and speakers. All come together to create new companies, jobs, and ideas, to learn and have fun.

This is a unique opportunity for you to connect and engage with the local community of passionate entrepreneurs and creative technologists.
